Rewards &
Sticker Economy

Kids earn stickers by completing habits and chores. Stickers are the currency of a parent-curated shop where real-world rewards — screen time, ice cream, movie picks — become achievable goals.

Legacy reference family-hub (v1) — for context only, not spec
  • Kids earned stickers (⭐ Gold Star, 💖 Heart, ✨ Magic, 🏆 Trophy) by placing them on weekly habit grids.
  • A sticker had a fixed AED value (1 sticker = AED 0.50); the rewards shop deducted from a "savings" pool, not the weekly pool directly.
  • Shop items ranged 5–50 stickers (Small Treat → Mystery Box); locked items shown greyed with a lock icon.
  • Close Week flow: Earn → Save / Cash Out / Claim / Invest in one modal; investments ran a 2× sticker return formula penalised by missed days.
  • Schema: habit_stickers, transaction_stickers, rewards, savings — all single-tenant, no RLS.
  • SaaS rebuild drops the AED layer, simplifies to sticker-only currency, adds per-member visibility, parent approval workflow, and multi-tenant RLS from day one.
